Do Nonfundamental Factors Matter to the Market?

Slightly! The market responds to sentiments, favorites, and economic keyword count and to lags of both fundamential and nonfundamental factors in the large-cap market; and mostly to positive-negative sentiments and favorites in the mid-cap and small-cap markets. Whereas low roc_auc_score maybe good news as the market overall is still efficient and unlikely tampered by influences, this model also shows an unbiased point given that the values in their accuracy matrices are balance between the two classifications.
